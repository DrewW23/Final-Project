What is Source Sounds?

Source Sounds is a music production company that specializes in providing the highest quality content for the best prices for our listeners and customers. I, Zach Smith, make most of the music that is used, or layer drums and other stuff on top of samples. We are open to making beats or any other type of music or instrumentation for you, if we do not have anything in our catalog that you like. Just feel free to reach out via the contact email or on one of the other social media platforms listed below, but, the business email is the best way to reach me or another member of the staff for special requests to be fulfilled.

How it all began

I have been deeply in love with music almost my whole life. And, I have recently set out to make my passion my job as well; making music and putting out my own albums. This is purely a passion project, but I do have a huge vision for this and I hope you come along for the ride with me as I upload songs, samples, and other things along the way that I have in store that I am cooking up behind the scenes.
